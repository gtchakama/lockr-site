Get Started View on GitHub

➜ ~ lockr set stripe_key sk_test_123

Successfully saved 'stripe_key' to group 'default'

➜ ~ lockr get stripe_key

sk_test_123

➜ ~ lockr export default

STRIPE_KEY=sk_test_123

Designed for Developers

Local-First Storage

All secrets are stored directly on your machine. No cloud sync, no accounts, zero external dependencies.

Encryption by Default

Secured with AES-256-GCM encryption and Argon2id key derivation. Your master password never leaves your memory.

Group-based Organization

Namespace your secrets cleanly using slashes (e.g., work/aws_key) to keep personal and professional credentials separated.

Environment Variable Export

Instantly pipe your secrets directly into your shell or local dev environment without copying and pasting.
